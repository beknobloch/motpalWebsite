How to Play

M.O.T.P.A.L., or "Many of These People Are Lying," is a talking game based on "Two of These People are Lying," a similar talk game created by Tom Scott and the Technical Difficulties.

Play begins by selecting one person in your group to be the "guesser." It does not matter who starts, as everyone will get a turn to be guesser. All players who are not the guesser should then do the following:
  1. Access a random, obscure Wikipedia page using Wikipedia's "Random Page" feature. The nature of the article must not be clearly discernible from the name of the article.
  2. Once all non-guessers have selected an article, use a tool to choose one of you at random.
All of the above can be accomplished on this site's appropriate page.

The guesser can use this page.
